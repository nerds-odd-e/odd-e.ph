Course Description

This three half-day Certified ScrumMaster® course covers the what, how, and especially the whys of the Scrum framework for product development--the theory and guidance on how to practice Scrum well.

The course is structured around stories and discussions, and is targeted to anyone who wants a deep understanding of Scrum. This includes Development Team members, Product Owners, Scrum Masters, and other stakeholders. Most of the course is given from the Scrum Master's perspective.

The course introduces us to why we want to deliver a product or service in small increments and how Scrum helps us do that. This then leads us into how we work together to be able to deliver the most valuable product in small increments. Throughout the course we shall expand our understanding to include common impacts to the larger organization when using Scrum.

Having basic knowledge of Scrum is expected and some experience is preferred. At a minimum, suggested reading is the Scrum Guide.

The course covers the following topics, depending on class pace:

  • Presence
  • Simulation: Using Scrum in Product Development
  • Scrum Team
  • Product Owner and Developers
  • Scrum Master
  • Event/Game Design
  • Engagement and Agility
  • Actions: Taking Scrum back to your organization
