Course Description

The world of work is transforming, becoming increasingly complex and uncertain. Organizations and leaders must deliver more innovative products and services on a more frequent basis in order to survive.

The Scrum Alliance Certified Agile Leadership (CAL) credential is an education and practice-based programme with the goal of developing Agile leadership competency and maturity in order to increase the effectiveness of a leader in these rapidly changing conditions.

By attending and successfully completing the CAL1 workshop you earn the Certified Agile Leadership credential.

Though each Certified Agile Leadership I course varies depending on the instructor, all Certified Agile Leadership courses intend to create awareness of, and begin the journey toward, Agile Leadership. Graduates will receive the Certified Agile Leadership I designation.

Who Should Attend

Managers, leaders, decision makers, consultants and coaches who assist with process and culture change in organizations will get the most from this workshop.

The following kinds of students gain the most from attendance:

  • Executives and business leaders
  • Managers of departments and teams
  • Coaches, consultants, and facilitators
  • Organizational Development Professionals
