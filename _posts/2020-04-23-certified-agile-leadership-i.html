Course Description

The world of work is transforming, becoming increasingly complex and uncertain. Organizations and leaders must deliver more innovative products and services on a more frequent basis in order to survive.

The Scrum Alliance Certified Agile Leadership (CAL) credential is an education and practice-based programme with the goal of developing Agile leadership competency and maturity in order to increase the effectiveness of a leader in these rapidly changing conditions.M

By attending and successfully completing the CAL1 workshop you earn the Certified Agile Leadership credential.
