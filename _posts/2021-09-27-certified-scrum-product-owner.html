Course Description

This three half-day Certified Scrum Product Owner® course covers the what, how, and especially the whys of the Scrum framework for product development, with a strong focus on the Scrum Product Owner role.

The course is structured around stories and discussions, and is targeted to anyone aiming for a Scrum Product Owner role or is involved in Product definition and direction, with an interest in Scrum.

The course introduces us to why we want to deliver a product or service in small increments and how Scrum helps us do that. This then leads us into learning from our customers and users, and using this insight to better drive product development. Throughout the course we shall expand our understanding to include common impacts to the larger organization when using Scrum.

Having basic knowledge of Scrum is expected and some experience is preferred. At a minimum, suggested reading is the Scrum Guide.

We shall cover the following topics during this course:

  • Presence
  • Simulation: Using Scrum in Product Development
  • Working with the Product Backlog
  • Understanding the Role of the Product Owner
  • Scrum Overview
  • Testing Product Assumptions
  • Dividing Value
  • Understanding Customers and Users
  • Describing Purpose and Strategy
  • Actions: Taking Scrum back to your organization
