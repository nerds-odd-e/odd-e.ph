Modern Agile Engineering Practices

Course Summary

In order to learn feedback fast, we need to have a shippable product increment every iteration. This requires changes to the organization and development practices, as the timeframe is too short for traditional practices. Modern development practices are necessary for organizational agility and for competing in our world today.

This workshop focuses on essential Agile engineering practices for individual software developers and for development teams. These practices enhances our Agility, and allows us to develop software better.

This is an intensive workshop, with most of the time spent on programming activities. We learn best by doing, and this workshop emphasizes this to provide participants a better understanding of modern Agile engineering practices.

This workshop is two (2) days. Workshop starts at 9:00 in the morning and ends at around 5:00 - 5:30 in the afternoon.
