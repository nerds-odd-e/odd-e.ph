Agile Deep Dive

Course Summary

The Agile Deep Dive program is a comprehensive six-day workshop that starts teams and organizations on being Agile. The program combines both a training component and a coaching component to better instill what Agile is, how a Scrum Team looks like, and experience how working in an Agile organization is like.

The program aims to:

  • Provide an overview of Agile Software Development and the Scrum Framework through the one-day Introduction to Scrum Workshop
  • Form a real Scrum Team: a Product Owner, a Development Team (composed of Software Developers, Software Testers, Analysts, and other roles necessary), with the Scrum Master role taken by the Odd-e Agile Coach
  • Deepen the understanding of Scrum by doing a real one-week sprint, facilitated and coached by the Odd-e Agile Coach acting as the Scrum Master
  • Showcase what a typical Agile workspace is, and the experience of working in a Scrum Team and Agile organization

The Agile Deep Dive program combines our Introduction to Scrum workshop, Agile Coaching, and elements of the our Stories to Specifications and Modern Agile Engineering Practices workshops into a cohesive whole.
